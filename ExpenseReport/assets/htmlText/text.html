What's possible on the BlackBerry10 platform for Enterprise?

The Submit Expense and Expense Report applications work together to allow seamless managing of expense submissions. First, the employee uses the Submit Expense application to create an expense report, attach their receipts as images, and send their expenses. The Expense Report application allows the manager to see, to approve or to reject the submissions. The manager can also invoke BBM to discuss directly with the employee.
